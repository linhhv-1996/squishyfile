# Contact Us

Hi there! I'm **J. Julian**, the creator behind Squishyfile. 

I built this platform because I was tired of file compression tools that were either too slow, required sketchy account sign-ups, or forced you to upload sensitive documents to unknown servers. My goal with Squishyfile is simple: to provide fast, private, and high-quality video and PDF compression that just works.

Since I run and maintain this project independently, I always love hearing from the people who use it. Your feedback is exactly what helps me improve the tools, fix annoying bugs, and keep everything running smoothly.

### Reporting a Bug?
To help me figure out the issue faster, it would be awesome if you could include:
* The specific tool you were using (e.g., Video Compressor, PDF Compressor).
* Your device and browser (e.g., iPhone 15 on Safari, Windows 11 on Chrome).
* A brief description of what went wrong (e.g., "The compression got stuck at 90%").

*Note: I try my absolute best to read and reply to every single email, but since I'm a solo developer, please allow 24 to 48 hours for a response. Thanks for using and supporting Squishyfile!*
