## What Makes a Genuinely Free Video Compressor

A truly free compressor has three properties:

**1. No watermark on the output**
Your compressed video is yours, clean and ready to share. A watermark makes the file unusable for any real purpose — personal or professional.

**2. No mandatory upload**
Server-based tools require your video to travel to their infrastructure before anything happens. For a 500MB file on a home connection, that's 5–10 minutes of upload time before compression even starts — and your footage is now on someone else's server.

**3. No account required**
No email, no password, no credit card. Open the tool, compress your video, close the tab.

Our compressor delivers all three. Processing happens entirely in your browser — your video never leaves your device.

## Privacy: Your Video Stays on Your Device

This isn't a marketing line — it's how the tool actually works.

Browser-based compression using WebAssembly means:
- No network request transmits your video
- No server ever receives your file
- No third party can access your footage
- Your video exists only on your device throughout the entire process

For most videos this doesn't change anything practical. But for footage that includes private individuals, minors, business-sensitive content, legal material, or anything you'd be uncomfortable uploading to a stranger's server — the difference matters.

Server-based tools have privacy policies. Policies are promises. The only real guarantee is that the video was never transmitted in the first place.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Why is my compressed file larger than expected?**
Very short videos (under 30 seconds) that were already well-compressed may not reduce much further. In these cases, the original is fine to use.

**Can I compress a video on my phone without an app?**
Yes. Open the tool in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android). Everything runs in the browser — no download needed.

**Is there a file size limit?**
We don't impose one. Very large files (3GB+) may take several minutes depending on your device's CPU and RAM, but they'll process.

**Can I use this commercially?**
Yes. The output has no watermark, no embedded metadata from our tool, and no attribution requirements.

**What happens to my video data after compression?**
Nothing — it exists in your browser's memory until you download it. When you close the tab, it's gone. We have no access to it at any point.

**Does the tool work on older devices?**
It works on any device running a modern browser. Older CPUs will be slower but will still complete the compression.
