# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 7, 2026

At **Squishyfile**, your privacy is our top priority. Our tool is designed to process files directly in your web browser (Client-side), ensuring your data stays under your control.

## 1. Local File Processing (Zero-Upload)
Squishyfile operates on a "Privacy-First" architecture. 
* **No Server Transfers:** Your files are **NEVER** uploaded, stored, or processed on our servers. 
* **Local Execution:** All compression and conversion tasks happen entirely on your local device. 
* **Instant Volatility:** Once you close the browser tab or clear the file, all data is immediately and permanently removed from your browser's memory.

## 2. Analytics & Data Collection
We use **Google Analytics** to monitor website performance and improve user experience. This service collects non-personal, anonymous information such as:
* Browser and device type.
* Referral sources and pages visited.
This data is used strictly for technical optimization and does not include any information from the files you process.

## 3. Advertising & Cookies
We use **Google AdSense** to display advertisements, which allows us to keep this tool free for everyone. 
* Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.
* Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
* You may opt out of personalized advertising at any time by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

## 4. Third-Party Links
Our website may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such external websites.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Effective Date."
